German IPTV – watch German television anywhere in the world

Living abroad and missing German TV? StreamDeutschland delivers German IPTV with a full channel lineup, live sport and 4K quality, wherever you have an internet connection.

What is German IPTV?

German IPTV delivers German television channels over the internet instead of satellite or cable. As your IPTV provider, once you subscribe you get login credentials for an app that streams live channels and on-demand content directly to your smart TV, phone, tablet or computer — no matter which country you're in.

What's included in a German IPTV package

  • All major German channels including ARD, ZDF, RTL, SAT.1, ProSieben and VOX
  • Live sport coverage, including Bundesliga, Sky Sport and Dazn
  • Over 50,000 live channels and 300,000+ movies and series on demand
  • 4K Ultra HD and Full HD picture quality with anti-freeze technology

Who uses German IPTV abroad?

Germany IPTV is popular with German expats, international workers on assignment, and viewers outside Germany who simply want access to German-language television and sport without needing a German address or satellite dish. It works the same way whether you're in another EU country or overseas.

German public television and regional channels

Every StreamDeutschland subscription includes all ARD channels (Das Erste, the regional third programmes), ZDF and its sister channels ZDFneo and ZDFinfo, Arte, Phoenix, and Kika. All channels are broadcast in HD where available. Regional news and programming from NDR, WDR, SWR and others are included at no extra cost.

International sports coverage in English and German

German IPTV includes full sports coverage: Bundesliga on Sky Sport, Champions League on DAZN and Sky, Formula 1 on Sky Sport F1, and Eurosport 1 and 2 for tennis, cycling and winter sports. Many of these channels offer English-language commentary options via second audio tracks.

Getting started

Setup takes just a few minutes: choose the number of devices and subscription length that suits your household, contact us on WhatsApp to complete your order, and receive your login details right away. Device compatibility

Our German IPTV service works on Smart TVs (Samsung, LG), Android TV boxes, Amazon Fire TV Stick, smartphones and tablets (iOS & Android), PC/Mac, and MAG set-top boxes, using apps such as IPTV Smarters Pro, TiviMate, Smart IPTV and GSE Smart IPTV.

Apps that work with German IPTV

StreamDeutschland works with TiviMate, IPTV Smarters Pro, GSE Smart IPTV, VLC and Kodi on any device. The setup takes under 5 minutes: you receive your credentials, enter them into your chosen app, and the channel list loads automatically. No hardware is required beyond your existing devices.

VPN compatibility for watching abroad

If you're using a German IPTV subscription outside Germany and some channels are geo-blocked, a VPN with a German server solves the issue instantly. StreamDeutschland works with all major VPN providers. We recommend connecting to a German server before launching your IPTV app for the best experience.

Häufige Fragen

Can I watch German IPTV outside of Germany?

Yes, German IPTV works anywhere with a stable internet connection, regardless of your physical location.

Do I need a German address to subscribe?

No, you only need an internet connection and a compatible device; no German address or bank account is required.

Is a free trial available before I subscribe?

Yes, you can request a free trial via WhatsApp to test channel quality and stability before choosing a plan.

How do I set up German IPTV from outside Germany?

After subscribing, you receive your credentials via WhatsApp. Enter them into TiviMate or IPTV Smarters Pro on your device. If certain channels are geo-blocked in your country, connect to a German VPN server before launching the app.

Does German IPTV work in English-speaking countries like the USA or UK?

Yes — StreamDeutschland works anywhere in the world with a stable internet connection of at least 10 Mbit/s for HD or 25 Mbit/s for 4K. No regional restrictions apply to the subscription itself.
