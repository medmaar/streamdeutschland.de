How the IPTV trial works

Request your trial through our trial request form or directly via WhatsApp. You'll receive test credentials for a limited period, which work in any common IPTV app — IPTV Smarters Pro, TiviMate, Smart IPTV or GSE Smart IPTV — on Smart TVs, Fire TV Stick, Android boxes, phones and computers. Full channel access during your trial

The trial gives you access to the same channel lineup as a paid subscription: 50,000+ live channels including German public TV, international sports, news in multiple languages, and 300,000+ on-demand titles. There are no restricted channels or degraded quality during the trial period.

Supported apps and devices

Your trial works on all the same apps as a paid subscription: TiviMate, IPTV Smarters Pro, GSE Smart IPTV, VLC, Kodi and more. You can test it on your Fire TV Stick, Android phone, Smart TV or PC. We'll send you setup instructions for your preferred device along with your trial credentials.

What to check during your trial

Use the trial the way you'd actually watch: put on a live match at peak time on a weekend evening, jump between channels to feel the switching speed, and try the on-demand library. If the stream stays smooth when half of Germany is watching the same game, you've found a provider worth keeping.

Stream stability and buffering

The main thing to evaluate is whether streams run smoothly on your internet connection and device. Test during peak hours (weekday evenings 19:00–22:00) when network load is highest. If 4K streams run without interruption during this window, your setup is solid.

Channel availability for your interests

Use the trial to verify that the specific channels you care about are available – your local sports league, specific international news networks, or niche content. Browse the EPG (electronic programme guide) in your app to see what's scheduled over the next few days.

After the trial

If you're convinced, pick a plan that matches your household — from a single device for one month to five simultaneous devices for a full year, starting at €9. There's no automatic conversion from trial to paid plan; you decide actively, or simply let the trial expire.

How to make the most of your free trial

Test at peak times for an accurate picture

The most important test condition is peak viewing time: weekday evenings between 19:00 and 22:00 CET, and Saturday/Sunday afternoons during Bundesliga matchdays. If your streams run smoothly during these windows, you can be confident in the server's stability. Testing only at off-peak hours (e.g. Tuesday morning) gives an incomplete picture of real-world performance.

Check the channels that matter most to you

Use the trial to browse the full channel list and verify your priority content is available: your home country's news channels, specific sports leagues, children's programming, or on-demand series. The EPG (electronic programme guide) shows scheduled content for 7 days ahead — a good way to evaluate both the channel depth and the programming guide accuracy before committing.
